The Best Movies According to Argha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Batman Arkham Knights

"Arkham Knight" is a gritty, action-packed adventure game that concludes the Arkham trilogy.
Set in a post-apocalyptic Gotham City, Batman faces off against Scarecrow, who has unleashed a fear toxin upon the city,
forcing the Dark Knight to confront his own demons while battling a new threat.

Your Name

"Your Name" (君の名は, *Kimi no Na wa) is a 2016 Japanese animated film directed by **Makoto Shinkai**.
The story follows two teenagers, **Mitsuha** and **Taki**, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies
intermittently despite living in different parts of Japan. As they navigate each other's lives,
they develop a connection and work together to uncover a larger mystery involving a catastrophic event.
The film is known for its stunning animation, emotional storytelling, and themes of fate, love, and time.
It became a global hit and is one of the highest-grossing anime films of all time.

Weathering with You

"Weathering with You" (天気の子, *Tenki no Ko*) is a 2019 Japanese animated film directed by **Makoto Shinkai**,
the creator of *Your Name*. The story follows **Hodaka**, a high school runaway in Tokyo, who meets **Hina**,
a girl with the mysterious ability to control the weather. As they grow closer, they use her power to help others
in a city plagued by constant rain, but they soon realize that manipulating nature comes at a cost. The film blends themes of love,
sacrifice, and the environment with stunning animation, offering a heartfelt and visually captivating experience.
It received critical acclaim and was a box office success worldwide.
